CHARACTER

DESCRIPTION
Oggy

The show revolves around Oggy, a cat with a clumsy, carefree, and foolish personality. It focuses on how he is constantly bothered by three stubborn cockroaches who reside in his home and persistently chases them around trying to get rid of them. Despite being clumsy and foolish, he is consistent and works hard to eliminate those who continuously bother him in his home.
Dee Dee

One of the mischievous cockroaches causing chaos in Oggy’s house and the hungriest of them all is Dee Dee. He has a fierce but also goofy personality and is always looking for anything, whether it be edible or inedible.
Marky

Another one of the naughty cockroaches is Marky. Unlike the other two cockroaches, he focuses more on dating, girls, and his hobby of reading. He also likes to cause a mess in the house; however, he is also tactful and lazy, often not caring about what the other two do.
Joey

The brains of the cockroach trio is Joey. Being the smartest and rudest of the group, he usually leads their activities and plans to annoy Oggy. Among them, he is the most mischievous, always scheming pranks and jokes on Oggy.
Jack

One of the other cats in the show is Jack, the cousin and sidekick of Oggy. Alongside Oggy, he aims to get rid of the cockroaches in Oggy’s house and constantly tries to eliminate them. However, his personality is quite different from Oggy; Jack is often violent and quick-tempered.
